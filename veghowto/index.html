    Favourite YouTubers:
  • Jenny Mustard (beautifully shot minimalist vegan recipe-videos, along with lifestyle tips on how to life a sustainable minimalist life!)
  • Peaceful Cuisine (beautifully shot recipe-videos for healthy vegan, Japanese and asian food)
  • Hotforfood (creative vegan alternatives to traditionally non-vegan foods)
  • The Edgy Veg (deliciously unhealthy vegan versions of your favourite junk food)
  • Cheap Lazy Vegan (simple, inexpensive vegan meals)
  • Kicki Yang Zhang (lifestyle and vegan food blog)
  • Mommy Tang (vegan mukbang, with recipes included!)

      Films:
    • Cowspiracy (primarily focused on the environmental impact of meat eating)
    • Earthlings (This documentary takes you inside many slaughterhouses, and can be extremely graphic. It focuses on the ethical and environmental impact of meat eating)
    • Vegucated (a documentary about 3 people becoming vegan for 6 weeks)
    • Forks Over Knives (talks about the current state of the food industry and the health impacts of meat eating)
